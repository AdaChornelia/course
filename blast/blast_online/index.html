Using Blast online

Blast is one of the most used bioinformatics tools ever written. It allows to find similaroties between sequences of proteins or nucleotides, at a reasonable speed.!

Its ancestor, the fasta suite (like the format) used an algorithm clled smith-waterman. While very accurate, was very slow.

Blast is available opnline on the ncbi website at the following address https://blast.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/Blast.cgi

Let us try it out!
